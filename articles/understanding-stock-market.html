CRYPTO FUNDAMENTALS

Blockchain & Crypto Fundamentals

Published: Jan 2026 ‚Ä¢ Reading time: -- min

This article explains how blockchain technology works, why cryptocurrencies exist, and what beginners should understand before engaging with digital assets.

What Is Blockchain Technology?

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across a network of computers. Each transaction is verified and permanently stored.

Why Cryptocurrencies Exist

Cryptocurrencies were created to allow peer-to-peer value transfer without relying on traditional financial intermediaries like banks.

Bitcoin vs Ethereum

Bitcoin is primarily a store of value, while Ethereum enables programmable applications using smart contracts.

Risks and Volatility

Crypto markets are highly volatile. Prices can change rapidly due to regulation, speculation, or technological developments.

Key Takeaways

  • Blockchain is a decentralized ledger technology
  • Crypto enables peer-to-peer value transfer
  • Volatility is significantly higher than traditional assets
