STOCK MARKET BASICS

Understanding the Stock Market

This article explains how the stock market works, why companies go public, and how investors participate.

What is the Stock Market?

The stock market is a marketplace where investors buy and sell ownership shares of publicly traded companies.

Why Companies Issue Stocks

Companies issue stocks to raise capital for expansion, innovation, and growth.

How Prices Move

Stock prices fluctuate based on supply and demand, earnings expectations, economic data, and investor sentiment.

Key Takeaway

Stocks represent ownership. Understanding fundamentals helps reduce risk and improve decision-making.
