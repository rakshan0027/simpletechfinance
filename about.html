About SimpleTechFinance

SimpleTechFinance is a beginner-friendly website created to explain technology news and finance basics in simple, easy-to-understand language.

We believe that everyone should understand how technology and money affect daily life — without complicated terms or confusing explanations.

Our content is designed for students, beginners, and non-technical readers who want to learn step by step.

All articles are educational, neutral, and written to help readers gain knowledge — not financial advice.
